# US Brand Tracker Dashboard

## Product Purpose
An internal research website for the Shapermint US Brand Tracker. The dashboard turns quarterly brand tracker decks and raw tracker exports into a concise, interactive view of awareness, brand equity ownership, discovery channels, and strategic blockers.

## Primary Users
Research, brand, marketing, and leadership teams who need to understand whether Shapermint is gaining salience and whether it can own a distinctive equity against Spanx and Skims.

## Core Job
Help the team quickly answer: are we increasing unaided awareness, are we improving aided awareness efficiently, and are we building any ownable brand equities rather than remaining behind Spanx and Skims.

## Strategic Principles
- Evidence first: every charted value must come from a deck, raw tracker export, or embedded chart table.
- Do not invent data: values that are unreadable or only visible as unlabeled chart points are marked as partial or missing.
- Make the blocker impossible to miss: Shapermint awareness and equity gaps versus Spanx and Skims should be visible on the first screen.
- Keep it useful for quarterly updates: data is separated from presentation code and each metric carries source notes.

## Tone
Clear, candid, and analytical. The site should feel like a working command center rather than a marketing page.
